Delete Your CoHabit Account

If you would like to delete your CoHabit account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to ugkmen@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your account email address in the message

What happens next?

  • Your account and personal data will be deleted
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics purposes
  • Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe
